About Ethereal Strokes

Ethereal Strokes is a curated art platform featuring limited edition paintings and original works from artists around the world. We believe in making art accessible while supporting creators and their unique visions.

Best Seller

Starry Night

by Vincent van Gogh

"Starry Night" The painting is considered one of the most beloved works of art of all time. It was not recognized as a masterpiece during van Gogh's lifetime, and he himself felt it was a failure. The painting is based on van Gogh's observations, imagination, memories, and emotions.The steeple resembles those common in van Gogh's native Holland. The whirling forms in the sky match published astronomical observations of nebulae.
