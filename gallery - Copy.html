Featured Works

Man in a Bowler Hat

by René Magritte

"Man in a Bowler Hat" features a man in a bowler hat with his face partially obscured by an object, symbolizing the hidden layers of reality and perception.

The Cafe Terrace on the Place de Forum, Arles, At Night

by Vincent Van Gogh

"The Café Terrace on the Place du Forum, Arles, At Night" captures the warm glow of a café under a starry night sky evoking the charm and liveliness of a bustling evening in Arles.

Christ in the Storm on the Sea of Galilee

by Rembrandt van Rijn

"Christ in the Storm on the Sea of Galilee" portrays Jesus calming a storm, with dramatic contrasts of light and shadow emphasizing chaos and faith.

Intimacy

by Stephen Shortridge

"Intimacy" depicts a tender and romantic moment, blending impressionistic brushstrokes with warm tones to evoke deep emotional connection.

Self-Portrait with Death Playing the Fiddle

by Arnold Böcklin

"Self-Portrait with Death Playing the Fiddle" depicts the artist with Death as a skeletal figure, symbolizing the inevitability of mortality.

Sunset Over Riga

by Thomas Kinkade

"Sunset Over Riga" depicts a serene sunset over the city, with warm colors and glowing light creating a peaceful atmosphere.

The Girl With A Pearl Earring

by Mauritschuis, The Hague

"The Girl With A Pearl Earring" The painting has been called the "Dutch Mona Lisa", mostly due to the ambiguous expression of the subject. The painting is on display in Mauritschuis in The Hague, Netherlands.

Persistence Of Memory

by Salvador Dalí

"Persistence Of Memory" The Persistence of Memory depicts melting clock faces and is thought to have been inspired by the Theory of Relativity. The painting is displayed at the MoMA
